About KGM Immigration & Educational Consultants Pvt Ltd

KGM Immigration & Educational Consultants Pvt Ltd (KGMIEC) has been India’s leading provider of educational content development since 2011. KGMIEC focuses on the skill development and vocational training landscape, building a skilled manpower pool to meet global industry requirements. The company is also one of the largest skill providers, offering training in skill development, consulting, recruitment, data processing, and other related services.

Our educated and trained counsellors assist students in selecting the right destination and provide appropriate qualifications based on their previous eligibility and experience. We ensure flexibility, transparency, and timely, quality solutions for our customers. Sourcing candidates, assessing, training, certifying, and placement assessments are conducted regularly during the training.

“Your study, Your Future, Our Priority” is our aim. It appropriately reflects our attitude and approach that leads to better placement of the student in various service sectors.
